About Me

Hello, I'm Akash Bade

I founded Mushroom Seed Nepal and Research Center to make native mushrooms suitable for production. We were testing the mushrooms produced in Nepal to make them suitable for cultivation. We have been successful in doing so. With this organization, I want to promote agriculture and mushroom farming in the context of Nepal.
