The Magic Bus 142

15th September, 2022

[A Plot from Into The Wild (2007) ]

'Magic Bus'
The Fairbanks Bus 142 was an abandoned bus, which McCandless found in his adventure to Alaska. It was the bus which Chris used as a makeshift shelter until his death.

The 'Magic Bus' is a short plot from the movie Into The Wild, a 2007 film based on true story of Christopher Johnson McCandless. This movie is one of my favourate and of course, I would like to recommend you to watch this.



Alexander SuperTramp


Alexander Supertramp was a pseudonym name of Christopher, called by himself. He left his family and society in search of adventure and adopt the nature as his wealth. Chris was graduated from Enormy University in Georgia in 1990. Later in April 1992, his final adventure was hitchhiking to Alaska, where he settled in an abandoned bus which he named as 'Magic Bus'.

"Happiness is only real when shared"
-Christopher J. McCandless



Into The Wild [2007]

Into The Wild is a 2007 film directed by Sean Penn based on the true story of Alexander SuperTramp originally known as Christopher Johnson McCandless. This movie is based on the book 'Into The Wild' written by Jon Krakauer in January 1996. Emile Hirsch played as Christopher which I liked his role very much. The movie highlights all the events, adventures, and backstory of Christopher. Without any heavy spoilers, I would like to recommend you to watch it if you liked the genre.


For final words, I haven't written all the details and events related to the movie, Christopher and the book as it makes the blog more longer. I took a short and important plot and named it as 'Magic bus 142'.
